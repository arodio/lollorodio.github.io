Short Bio


I earned a BSc in Computer Engineering and an MSc in Telecommunications from Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy, in 2018 and 2020, respectively.
Through a double diploma program, I obtained an M.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Université Côte D'Azur, France.
I defended my PhD thesis on “Client Heterogeneity in Federated Learning Systems”, at Inria, France, on July 3rd, 2024, supervised by Dr. Giovanni Neglia.
