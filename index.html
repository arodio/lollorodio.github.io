About me


I am a postdoctoral researcher at Linköping University, Sweden, working with Prof. Erik G. Larsson and Prof. Zheng Chen on decentralized learning.

Previously, I completed my PhD at Inria, France, working with Dr. Giovanni Neglia on federated learning.

My research focuses on designing scalable distributed algorithms for efficient use of data and system resources. I employ mathematical tools from distributed optimization and statistical learning theory.
